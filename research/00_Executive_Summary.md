## Risk Analysis

### Market Risks
- **Risk:** Established competitors (Meltwater, Brandwatch) launch SME tiers
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Move fast, build on differentiation (European languages, bias transparency)

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** AI accuracy insufficient for user expectations
- **Likelihood:** Low-Medium
- **Mitigation:** Hybrid VADER+Gemini approach, continuous improvement, transparency about confidence

### Business Risks
- **Risk:** User acquisition too expensive (high CAC) or low conversion (churn)
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Product-led growth (low CAC freemium), strong onboarding (reduce churn), feedback loops

### Regulatory Risks
- **Risk:** GDPR violations, copyright issues with news scraping
- **Likelihood:** Low-Medium
- **Mitigation:** GDPR compliance Day 1, legal review of scraping, consider licensing agreements
